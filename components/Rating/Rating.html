Rating

Ratings provide insight regarding others' opinions and experiences, and can allow the user to submit a rating of their own.

Star Rating

Star Rating that you can embed in your website by using class "rating". 

    
//Star Rating:

<div class="rating">
<i class="fas fa-star rating-color"></i>
<i class="fas fa-star rating-color"></i>
<i class="fas fa-star rating-color"></i>
<i class="fas fa-star"></i>
<i class="fas fa-star"></i>
</div>

Sizes Rating

Different sizes of rating that you can use in you website by usind classes like "sm","lg","md" for small , large , medium respectively. 

    
//Sizes Rating:

<div class="rating">
<i class="fas fa-star rating-color sm"></i>
<i class="fas fa-star rating-color lg"></i>
<i class="fas fa-star rating-color md"></i>
</div>

Emoji Rating

Emoji Rating that you can use in your website. 

    
//Emoji Rating:

<div class="rating">
<i class="fas fa-frown-open emoji rating-color"></i>
<i class="fas fa-frown emoji rating-color"></i>
<i class="fas fa-meh emoji rating-color"></i>
<i class="fas fa-smile emoji"></i>
<i class="fas fa-laugh emoji"></i>
</div>
