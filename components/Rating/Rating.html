Rating

Ratings provide insight regarding others' opinions and experiences, and can allow the user to submit a rating of their own.

Star Rating

Star Rating that you can embed in your website by using class "rating". 

    
//Star Rating:

<div class="rating">
<i class="fas fa-star rating-color">
<i class="fas fa-star rating-color">
<i class="fas fa-star rating-color">
<i class="fas fa-star">
<i class="fas fa-star">
</div>

Sizes Rating

Different sizes of rating that you can use in you website by usind classes like "sm","lg","md" for small , large , medium respectively. 

    
//Sizes Rating:

<div class="rating">
<i class="fas fa-star rating-color sm">
<i class="fas fa-star rating-color lg">
<i class="fas fa-star rating-color md">
</div>

Emoji Rating

Emoji Rating that you can use in your website. 

    
//Emoji Rating:

<div class="rating">
<i class="fas fa-frown-open emoji rating-color">
<i class="fas fa-frown emoji rating-color">
<i class="fas fa-meh emoji rating-color">
<i class="fas fa-smile emoji">
<i class="fas fa-laugh emoji">
</div>
