Toast

Toast provide brief messages about app processes at the bottom of the screen.

Baseline Toast

This is a baseline Toast which you can embed at the bottom of your website.

Can't send photo.Retry in 5 seconds.

    
//Baseline Toast:

<div class="snackbar-container">
<div class="snackbar">
<p>Can't send photo.Retry in 5 seconds.

<button class="btn-retry">RETRY
<button class="btn-close">X
</div>
</div>

Leading Toast

This is a Leading Toast which you can embed at the bottom of your website.By default, snackbars are centered horizontally within the viewport. On larger screens, they can optionally be displayed on the leading edge of the screen

Your photo has been archived.

    
//Leading Toast:

<div class="snackbar-container">
<div class="snackbar">
<p>Your photo has been archived.

<button class="btn-retry">UNDO
<button class="btn-close">X
</div>
</div>

Stacking Toast

SThis is a stacking Toast which you can embed at the bottom of your website.Action buttons with long text should be positioned below the label instead of alongside it.

Your item already has label "travel".You can add a new label


//Stacking Toast:

<div class="snackbar-container">
<div class="snackbar2">
<p>Your item already has label "travel".You can add a new label

<div class="snackbar-btns">
<button class="btn-retry">ADD A NEW LABEL
<button class="btn-close">X
</div>
</div>
</div>
