Typography

Documentation and examples for common text utilities to control alignment, wrapping, weight, and more.

Heading

Different kinds of headings that you can embed in your website.

h1.heading 1

h2.heading 2

h3.heading 3

h4.heading 4

h5.heading 5
h6.heading 6

//Headings:
<h1>h1.heading 1</h1> 
<h2>h2.heading 2</h2>
<h3>h3.heading 3</h3> 
<h4>h4.heading 4</h4>
<h5>h5.heading 5</h5>
<h6>h6.heading 6</h6>

Different kinds of text alignment that you can use in your text.

Left Aligned Text

Center Aligned Text

Right Aligned Text


//Text Alignment:

<p class="left">Left Aligned Tex</p>  
<p class="center">Center Aligned T</p>

Capitalize Text

Lowercase Text

Uppercase Text

Light

Regular

Medium

500

Bold

small

medium
